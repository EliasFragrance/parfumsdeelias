RODAYO

Eau de Parfum · Night

RODAYO is a dark, polished night fragrance in matte black and gold. Built on warm woods, subtle sweetness, and a discreet smokiness, it’s made for dinners, lounges, and the hours after midnight – when the room is slower, the lights are lower, and details matter.

Concentration: Eau de Parfum Category: Evening / Signature Night

Drop I · 2025

Olfactory profile

Opening

Bright spice and a touch of citrus cut through the night air, introducing the scent without overpowering the room.

  • Spiced citrus
  • Clean aromatic notes

Heart

The fragrance settles into smooth woods and subtle florals, creating a refined warmth that sits close to tailored fabric.

  • Warm woods
  • Soft floral nuance

Base

A deep, lingering base of woods, resins, and a hint of sweetness leaves a quiet trail that feels expensive and controlled.

  • Dry woods
  • Resinous amber
  • Subtle vanilla tone

How to wear RODAYO

Occasions

Ideal for dinners, cocktail bars, lounges, late meetings, and any evening where the dress code is elevated and intentional.

Seasonality

Best in autumn, winter, and cooler spring nights – when the air gives the warmer accords room to breathe.

Application

3–5 sprays on pulse points (neck, behind ears, chest). Let the fragrance sit on skin – no rubbing – to preserve the structure.

The bottle

RODAYO is presented in a matte black rectangular flacon with a textured gold cap and engraved-style typography. The design references traditional European perfume houses, stripped back to a clean, masculine silhouette that lives comfortably on a nightstand or bar cart.
