The House

A House Built on Precision, Passion, and Purpose.

Parfums de Elias is a modern fragrance house rooted in the art of seduction and self-expression. We create scents for the bold, the driven, and the unapologetically ambitious.

Our Philosophy.

Our work begins long before a bottle is filled. From raw material selection to final maceration, every decision is made slowly, intentionally, and without compromise.

We believe a fragrance should do three things:

  • • Tell a story.
  • • Elevate your presence.
  • • Last from first impression to final moment.

That belief informs every formula, every bottle, and every release.

Craft

From Formula to Skin.

Each composition is built from high quality aroma-chemicals and naturals, balanced for clarity and performance. Concentrations are chosen not for cost savings but for the experience on skin.

We test our fragrances in real life — late nights, crowded rooms, open air — to ensure they perform where they matter most.
