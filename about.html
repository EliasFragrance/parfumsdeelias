About the house

The story behind Parfums de Elias

Parfums de Elias was created for the guy who outgrew body sprays and generic designer bottles, but still wanted scents that punch above their price – and match the man he’s becoming.

It started with self-improvement

The brand was founded by Alex, a business graduate and fragrance addict who was obsessed with one question:

“If I’m putting in work in the gym, in my style, in my career – why would my fragrance be an afterthought?”

Between late nights studying, travelling through Europe, and building businesses from his laptop, Alex noticed the same thing: the right scent changes how people remember you. But most fragrances either smelled like everyone else, or felt disconnected from how real guys actually live.

Parfums de Elias is his answer – a focused collection of modern fragrances built around real life: gym, work, dates, summers, and nights out.

Built from real life

Our vision

We’re not trying to be the biggest fragrance house in the world. We’re building something tighter: a small, serious lineup that covers your lifestyle without 20 bottles gathering dust.

Each release is designed with a specific moment in mind – night out, date night, summer, nautical – so you always know exactly when to wear it.

What we care about

Three things drive every decision we make:

  • Real performance. Fragrances that actually last and project – without choking the room.
  • Intentional design. Bottles and branding that look as good on your shelf as they smell on your skin.
  • Respect for the wearer. Transparent messaging, no fake hype, and scents that help you feel put-together, not insecure.

The collection

Our first drop, RODAYO, is a warm, confident night-out fragrance built for rooftop bars, lounges, and long nights.

Following RODAYO, we’re building a tight core lineup:

  • Casanova – a smooth, intimate date-night scent for close moments.
  • Nautique – a fresh, salty, clean scent inspired by the sea and travel.
  • Aventuro – a bright, energetic summer fragrance for hot days and vacations.

Together, they cover the main scenarios in your life – so you don’t waste time staring at a shelf full of almost-identical scents.

From idea to bottle

Parfums de Elias is still independent and founder-led. That means every creative decision – from the way the bottle looks to how the fragrance opens on skin – is intentional.

You’re not just buying a logo. You’re buying into a mindset: taking your life seriously, investing in your image, and smelling like you actually have your stuff together.
