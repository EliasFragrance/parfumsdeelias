FAQ

Questions, Answered.

Below are answers to the most common questions about our fragrances, shipping, and experience.

How long do your fragrances last?
Our extrait and eau de parfum concentrations are designed to provide 8–16 hours of performance depending on your skin chemistry and environment.
Are your products cruelty-free?
Yes. We do not test on animals, and we require the same commitment from our suppliers.
Where are your fragrances made?
All blends are formulated, macerated, and bottled in Canada under strict quality control.
Do you offer samples?
Sample sets are in development and will be released in limited batches. Join our email list to be notified first.
Can I return or exchange a fragrance?
Due to the nature of fragrance products, we can only accept returns on sealed, unused items. Please review our Shipping & Returns policy for full details.
